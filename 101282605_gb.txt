Information about George Brown College

George Brown College is located in the heart of the Toronto City. It offers variety of courses to the students across 3 
different campuses. It allows indigenous students as well as international students to dive deeper into their future and 
succeed into the career. George Brown College provides hands-on lab infrastructure with lectures, which contain great 
amount of knowledge, so students can get amazing skill by attending it regularly. GBC has majority of students who are 
doing part-time job with their full-time study. Students can do on-campus part-time job with their studies. Several 
student are willing to do volunteer work in a college.
