Information about George Brown College

George Brown College is located in the heart of Toronto City. It offers a variety of courses to the students across 3 
different campuses. It allows indigenous students as well as international students to dive deeper into their future and 
succeed in their careers. George Brown College provides hands-on lab infrastructure with lectures, which contain a great 
amount of knowledge, so students can get amazing skills by attending it regularly. GBC has a majority of students who are 
doing a part-time job with their full-time study. Students can do the on-campus part-time job with their studies. Several 
students are willing to do volunteer work in a college.
